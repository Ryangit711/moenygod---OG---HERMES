## The Core Idea

Money and debt are **not natural** — they are human-made systems of obligation. The word "economy" originally meant **household management** (oikonomia), not GDP growth. Modern economics has replaced stewardship with accumulation (chrematistike). The people who truly understand this use that knowledge to navigate — and sometimes profit from — the system's crises, while the masses remain convinced that the economy is as natural and inevitable as weather.

**The question is never "what will the market do?" The question is always "who manages the household, and for whom?"**
