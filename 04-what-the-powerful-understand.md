## The Debt Jubilee: An Ancient Solution

Ancient societies understood that unpayable debt would eventually consume everything. Their solution: **the debt jubilee** — a periodic cancellation of all debts.

- **Sumer and Babylon** (~2400–1900 BC): Rulers like Enmetena and Hammurabi issued *andurarum* decrees cancelling debts and freeing debt slaves
- **Ancient Israel**: The Sabbath year (every 7th year) and Jubilee year (every 50th year) cancelled debts and restored land to original families
- **Purpose**: Prevent wealth concentration, maintain social stability, keep the household (oikos) viable

These were not acts of charity. They were acts of **political survival**. When too many people fall into debt peonage, the system collapses. The rulers understood this. Modern rulers often do not — or choose to serve the creditors instead of the household.
